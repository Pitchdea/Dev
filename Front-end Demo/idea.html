I want to teach parrots to talk english.

I want to start a speech clinic that teaches parrots how to talk english. I am a ph.D. of neuroscience and have studied parrot speech impediments and vocal biology. My wish is to start a clinic where you can take your bird for weekly sessions to teach it to talk.

My studies show that with the correct teaching parrots learn remarkably fast and accurately. With the methods I'm sharing almost every parrot species is able to learn the basic vocabulary. Learning of the basics takes only two weeks! You could have your own talking parrot in no time. Did you know that your regular house parrot can learn the vocabulary of up to 80 words and recognize even long sentences?

My main point of focus is to find out if I have a market big enough to support the clinic's existence. I am also looking for suitable places and some workforce at this moment. All help is welcome, you can find the question and my contacts below.
